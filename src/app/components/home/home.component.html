About GTA-Utah

Starting from the aspirations of the NRI's to unite the entire Telangana community, with a strong will, the seeds of "Vishwavedika" has been planted to ensure everyone comes together. Global Telangana Association vision is to become a volunteer driven organization that operates with the most transparent way to support the development activities in Telangana region and USA.
